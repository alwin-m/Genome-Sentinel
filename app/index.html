Welcome to Genome Sentinel

A complete molecular docking workspace for drug discovery research. This guide walks you through the 6-step workflow.

1

Select a Protein Target

Choose a disease target from curated presets (Alzheimer's, Malaria, Diabetes, Cancer) or enter a custom PDB ID to download from RCSB.

2

Prepare Ligand Library

Generate 3D structures of FDA-approved drugs automatically from SMILES notation. Preset ligands are available for each disease target.

3

Configure Docking Grid

Define the 3D search box around the protein's active site. Presets auto-load known coordinates; manual adjustment is supported.

4

Run Docking Simulations

Select a prepared protein and ligand, then launch AutoDock Vina. Watch real-time log output as the simulation runs.

5

Analyze Results in 3D

View ranked binding affinities in a table. Load any docked complex into the interactive 3D viewer to inspect poses.

6

Generate Research Paper

Automatically compile your experimental data into a formatted markdown manuscript with methods, results, and references.
